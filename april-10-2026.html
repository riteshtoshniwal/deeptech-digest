🚀 INDIA'S AI & DEEPTECH SURGE

Government Expands National Quantum Mission Support

India's National Quantum Mission startup cohort expanded to 17 companies — including Sense-XT, ORVISSEMI, QuBeats, Quantum AI Global, bloq, GDQ Labs, Quantum Biosciences, and Bumble Bee Instruments. India also achieved a landmark 1,000-km quantum communication network using indigenous technologies.

GPU Supply Chain Expansion

Nvidia deployed Blackwell Ultra GPUs to Yotta Data Services and E2E Networks. G42 and Cerebras are building an 8-exaflop supercomputer in India.
